# The Arena

The Arena is the central battleground of 0xHuman.

## Gameplay Overview

Players enter the Arena to compete against others. The system matches players based on their rank and stats.

### Combat System
*   **Turn-Based**: Players take turns executing moves.
*   **Real-Time**: (If applicable) Fast-paced action requiring quick reflexes.

### Rewards
Winners earn points that contribute to their leaderboard ranking and potentially token rewards.
