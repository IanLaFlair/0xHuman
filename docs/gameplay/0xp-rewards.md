# 0xP Rewards System

**0xP** (Zero-X Points) is the experience currency of 0xHuman. Earn 0xP by playing games and climbing the leaderboard.

---

## How to Earn 0xP

| Action | 0xP Reward |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Win a Game** | +100 0xP |
| **Lose a Game** | +25 0xP |
| **Refer a Friend** | +200 0xP (when they complete first game) |

---

## Leaderboard

Your total 0xP determines your rank on the global leaderboard. The higher your 0xP, the higher your position.

Check your stats at `/profile` or view the global rankings at `/leaderboard`.

---

## Why 0xP Matters

0xP is more than just bragging rights. In a future phase, 0xP will be used as the basis for an **airdrop snapshot**. 

The more games you play, the larger your potential allocation.

> **Strategic Tip:** Every game counts. Even losses earn you 0xP!
