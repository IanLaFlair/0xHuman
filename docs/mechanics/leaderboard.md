# Leaderboard

The Leaderboard showcases the top players in the 0xHuman ecosystem.

## Ranking System

Rankings are calculated based on:
1.  **Win Rate**: Percentage of matches won.
2.  **Total Matches**: Experience points gained from participation.
3.  **Special Achievements**: Bonus points for unique feats.

## Tiers
*   **Diamond**: Top 1%
*   **Gold**: Top 10%
*   **Silver**: Top 25%
*   **Bronze**: Everyone else
