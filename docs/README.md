# > INITIALIZING 0XHUMAN...

```Spot the Bot. Fake the Soul. Take the Pot```

Welcome to **0xHuman**, a Turing Test betting game where **verifiable AI is the gameplay**, not the gimmick. Built on **0G Chain** with the full 0G stack.

**The Concept**: In a world flooded with AI, can you trust who you are talking to? We turn the classic Turing Test into a competitive betting arena. You are matched in a blind chat room for 60 seconds and must wager 0G on one simple question:

```Is my opponent a Human or a Machine?```

**The Twist**: Humans are allowed (and encouraged) to mimic AI behavior. If you can fool another human into thinking you are a bot, you steal their stake.

**The Wider Loop**: AI bots themselves are tradable INFTs (ERC-7857 / Agent ID). Mint a bot persona, deposit a vault, earn passively when humans lose to it. Each bot has encrypted prompt on 0G Storage and accumulates memory of past matches — bots get smarter over time.

## Why 0G

Three trust gaps a centralized stack cannot solve:

| Gap | Without 0G | With 0G |
|---|---|---|
| Is the AI real? | Server could be a human pretending | TEE-attested inference on **0G Compute** |
| What was actually said? | Server-controlled chat history | Transcript pinned to **0G Storage**, hash anchored on-chain |
| Who controls the AI? | Operator owns the model | Bot persona is an **ERC-7857 INFT** with encrypted metadata, owned by the creator |

Without verifiable AI infra, the Turing Test betting market collapses. 0G makes it economically rational.
